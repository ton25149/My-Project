Premier League Tour

The Premier League, often referred to as the English Premier League, is the highest level of football competition in the English Football League system. The 20 clubs have a promotion and relegation system with the English Football League (EFL). The season runs from August to May, each team playing 38 home and away matches. Most matches are played on Saturday and Sunday afternoons (local time).
